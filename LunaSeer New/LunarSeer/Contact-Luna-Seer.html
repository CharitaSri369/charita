Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our readings and services.

Before your reading, take some time for quiet reflection. Consider the questions or areas of your life you'd like guidance on. Arrive with an open mind and heart. It's helpful to have specific questions prepared, but also be open to receiving unexpected insights. You don't need to bring anything special, just yourself and your curiosity.

Our standard reading sessions last approximately 60 minutes, which includes time for your questions and discussion of the insights revealed. We also offer 30-minute focused readings for specific questions, and 90-minute in-depth sessions for more complex situations or comprehensive life path guidance. The duration will be confirmed when you book your appointment.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay for online bookings. Payment is required at the time of booking to secure your virtual appointment. Gift certificates are also available for purchase if you'd like to gift a reading to someone special.

We understand that circumstances change. You may reschedule or cancel your appointment with at least 24 hours notice without any charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will incur a 50% fee. No-shows or cancellations within 2 hours of the appointment will be charged the full session fee. In case of emergencies, please contact us as soon as possible to discuss options.

All our readings are conducted virtually via Zoom, FaceTime, or phone call. Our virtual sessions are powerful and deeply insightful, as the energy and connection transcend physical distance. After booking, you'll receive detailed instructions on how to prepare your space and connect for your session. We recommend finding a quiet, private space where you won't be interrupted.

The frequency of readings depends on your personal journey and needs. Many clients find quarterly readings helpful to stay aligned with seasonal energies and life changes. For specific situations or periods of significant transition, monthly readings may be beneficial. We recommend allowing time between readings to integrate the guidance received and observe how situations unfold. Our seers can provide personalized recommendations based on your circumstances.
